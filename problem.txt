Xij (true if vertice i has color j)
Yj	(total points of a color)
C 	(points of the color with max points (will be minimized))
Wi  (points of vertice i)


min C

subject to


//All nodes have a single color
1. for all j in COLORS:
	(SUM Xij) = 1

//All adjacent nodes must have different colors
2. for all u,v in VERTICES and j in COLORS:
	Xuj + Xvj <= 1

//Points of color i
3.for all j in COLORS:
	for all i in VERTICES:
		Yj += Xij*Wi 

4. for all j in COLORS:
	C >= Yj


Xij {0,1} Yj {0,1}
